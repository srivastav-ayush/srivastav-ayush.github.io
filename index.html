About Me

I am currently working as an Assitant Manager in System Integration at Ola Electric. I work with battery packs, li-ion cells and Battery Management System (BMS).

I completed my undergraduate studies in Mechanical Engineering from MANIT, Bhopal, India. I have worked with the Biomechanics Research Unit at KU Leuven, Belgium under the guidance of Prof. Liesbet Geris. We studied the surgerical treatment of Osteoarthritis by designing a FEA model to simulate the regeneration of articular cartilage inside scaffolds. I have also worked with the Socket Sense research group at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden under the guidance of Prof. Dejiu Chen. My research involved data-driven analysis of transfemoral amputees to design personalised comfortable prosthetic sockets.

I have experience working in Li-ion cells, Soft Matter, Biomechanics, Solid Mechanics, Numerical Simulations, Finite Element Analysis and Machine Design.

In my free time, I enjoy reading books and writing their reviews, playing badminton, and swimming. Underwater life forms pique my curiosity. I am thrilled by adventure sports and delighted by gourmet cuisines.
