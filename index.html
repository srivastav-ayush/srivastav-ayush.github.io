Recuperation of Scaphoid Bone Fracture

08/2020 - Present

Scaphoid bone is the most vulnerable bone of the 8 carpal bones in our hands. Its fractures are mistaken by the patients as mild pain, due to its complicated blood circulation pattern and slow healing.

  • Reconstructed the Scaphoid bone as a CAD model from an STL File, generated from CT scan of a cadaver’s bone.
  • Predicted the fracture patterns at different impact loads in FEA using ANSYS static structural analysis.
  • Working on predictive surgery i.e., precisely locating the K-wire and Herbert’s screw fixation in the best configuration for maximum stability and faster healing.

eBAJA - All Terrain Electric Vehicle

03/2019 - 01/2020

Worked on designing the steering system of the eBAJA buggy

  • Produced a peak torque of 38 Nm and maximum acceleration of 3.75 m/s2 at nominal current of 100 A. Reaching top speed of 38 km/h with run time of 108 min in a full charge.
  • Used Reverse Ackerman geometry to minimize bump steer, avoid buckling of tie rod and increase pedal installation space.
  • Achieved an Ackerman percentage of 104% with a steering ratio of 128 mm/rev for a turning radius of 1.5m.
