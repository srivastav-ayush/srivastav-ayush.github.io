About Me

I am a Manager in System Integration at Ola Electric, where I specialize in vehicle-level performance and range simulations, developing lumped parameter thermal models for sub-system temperature predictions, vehicle thermal management and conducting lithium-ion cell/battery data analytics. My work focuses on optimizing automotive performance and efficiency through computational modeling and data-driven insights.

I hold a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from NIT Bhopal, where I worked under the guidance of Dr. R.K. Dwivedi, Dr. Vinod Yadav and Dr. Deepak Kumar. My academic research spanned soft matter, biomedical engineering, automotive design and manufacturing technology with a particular focus on advancements in computational modeling & simulation. For my bachelor thesis, I worked with the Socket Sense research group at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, where I conducted data-driven analysis of transfemoral amputees to design personalized, comfortable prosthetic sockets.

With both industry and research experience, I bring expertise in automotive performance, control strategy design, computational modeling, and bio-medical engineering. You can find my detailed CV here.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading and reviewing books, running, trekking, swimming, badminton and discovering gourmet restaurants in the city.
